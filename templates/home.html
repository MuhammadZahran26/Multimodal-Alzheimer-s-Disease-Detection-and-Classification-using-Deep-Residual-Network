Core Platform Features

Tools for patients and doctors to upload scans, understand results, and follow progress over time.

Patient Management

Keep all patient information in one place — demographics, medical notes, visits, and scan results.

  • • Easy-to-read patient timeline with visits and updates.
  • • Add notes, files, and images to each visit.
  • • Only authorized staff can view or edit patient data.

Progress Monitoring

See how a patient’s condition changes over time with charts, old scan results, and side-by-side comparisons.

  • • Patients can view all previous scans and test results.
  • • Doctors can review complete patient history and compare scans easily.
  • • Alerts if results show a sudden drop or worrying change.

AI Assistant

An assistant that explains disease details, answers questions, and guides patients — from prevention tips to simple summaries.

  • • Explains Alzheimer’s symptoms and progression.
  • • Offers prevention advice, exercises, and resources.
  • • Helps prepare referral notes for clinicians.

Multi-Format Support

Upload and view DICOM, NIfTI, PNG, and JPEG files without extra tools.

  • • Works with medical scan formats and normal images.
  • • View slices, metadata, and image details easily.
  • • Optional removal of personal details for privacy.

Privacy & Security

All data is encrypted and protected to keep patient information safe.

  • • Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • • Audit logs show who accessed what and when.
  • • User roles & permissions prevent unauthorized access.

Recommendation system

Personalized referrals and nearby care suggestions — helps patients find the right hospital and specialist, fast.

  • • Suggests nearby hospitals and specialists based on location and clinical need.
  • • Matches patient condition to recommended care pathways and clinicians.
  • • Filters by insurance, language, and availability for practical referrals.
  • • Option to send recommended referrals directly to a doctor or patient inbox.

Why MediCare AI focuses on Alzheimer’s

Early detection matters. Alzheimer’s disease begins long before symptoms are obvious — and early intervention yields the best outcomes. MediCare AI helps clinicians and patients spot patterns, track change over time, and provide actionable next steps.
